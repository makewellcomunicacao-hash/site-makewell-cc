Why a proprietary framework?

Most agencies execute. Few think. The Makewell Framework exists because marketing without methodology is luck. And luck is not a business model.

Over 9 years, we tested what works and what wastes client money. In the beginning, we were a creative agency. Then we learned how to do traffic. Then we understood that traffic without CRM is like a leaky bucket. Then we realized that all of it fails if positioning is generic.

The Makewell Framework is the result of that journey: a methodology that doesn't treat branding, content, traffic and sales as separate services — it treats them as gears of a single predictable revenue engine.

If each gear runs at a different agency (or in departments that don't talk inside the same company), the engine stalls. That's why our clients have everything integrated internally: strategy, creation, paid media and CRM under one roof, moving in the same direction.

Pillar 01 · Foundation

Positioning & Branding

Build the brand before amplifying

Before investing a single dollar in media, we define who the brand is, who it speaks to, what promise it sustains, and how it differentiates. Amplifying a confused brand only accelerates the confusion. And burns budget.

We work on strategic positioning, verbal architecture, visual identity and brand manifesto as the foundation everything else rests on.

Makewell ApplicationA brand that knows what it is attracts who it should attract and repels who it should repel. This reduces CAC and increases margin.
Pillar 02 · Voice

Content & Authority

Produce what builds reputation and converts

Content at Makewell is born from a proprietary framework that crosses the greatest minds in advertising (Ogilvy, Sinek, Godin, Olivetto) with applied neuromarketing and modern conversion engineering.

We produce what educates the market, builds authority and prepares the ground for sales, all anchored in the positioning defined in Pillar 01.

Makewell ApplicationEvery piece of content has a role in the funnel: attract, engage, convert.
Pillar 03 · Distribution

Traffic & Paid Media

Predictable acquisition of qualified audience

Organic is a short blanket. Paid media is what turns content into pipeline. We structure full-funnel campaigns on Meta, Google, TikTok and YouTube, always connected to the previous pillars.

We don't run traffic to generate likes. We run it to generate qualified leads, with predictable CAC and full traceability through to closing.

Makewell ApplicationEvery dollar invested is measured against return. ROI +26x in Legal and +20x in Healthcare are results of method, not luck.
Pillar 04 · Revenue

Sales & CRM

Transform interest into contracted revenue

Where most agencies disconnect, Makewell starts the most important work. We structure the CRM, train the sales team in SPIN Selling, automate follow-up and measure the journey from first click to payment.

This is the pillar that closes the cycle: without it, everything that comes before becomes cost, not investment.

The difference in practice

What changes when a brand switches from the logic of "hiring marketing services" to the logic of "operating within an integrated framework".

Traditional Model

Agency hires parts

  • Branding at one agency, traffic at another, CRM with the client itself
  • Each vendor optimizes their own KPI, not the business
  • Weekly meetings become blame ping-pong
  • Pretty content that doesn't convert, expensive traffic that doesn't close
  • Impossible to track real return on investment
Makewell Framework

Integrated ecosystem

  • The 4 pillars operated by the same team, under the same strategy
  • All KPIs point to a single objective: client revenue
  • Meetings discuss results, not work defense
  • Content built to convert, traffic built to close
  • Single dashboard from first click to signed contract

Who the Framework makes sense for

The Makewell Framework is for brands that treat communication as strategic investment, not cosmetic expense. Typically:

Companies in repositioning or expansion phases that need to consolidate authority, scale with method and move beyond guesswork.

High-ticket independent professionals — physicians, lawyers, dentists, engineers, consultants — who depend on built authority and qualified leads, not inflated reach.

Industries that need to build a B2B brand, generate qualified leads and shorten the sales cycle with integrated strategic communication.

NGOs & Third Sector: impact organizations that need to build narrative, engage donors and scale their cause with the same rigor as a market brand. This is what we did with Casa Hunter.
