Our story

Makewell was born in 2017, in São Paulo (SP), with a clear purpose: to turn businesses into brands with soul, direction and purpose. From 2020 on, we expanded operations to the Northeast and established headquarters in Maceió (AL), keeping São Paulo as a strategic operation. In the beginning, we were an agency like any other. We produced art, wrote posts, ran traffic. What pulled us out of that category was discovering, year after year, that content without method is just noise.

Over nearly a decade, we built an ecosystem that brings together what most agencies treat as separate departments: brand positioning, content creation, paid traffic, automation, CRM and sales process. Today we operate from Maceió and São Paulo, serving brands across Brazil and the United States.

Our cases carry the numbers that explain why our clients stay: +26x ROI in the legal niche, +20x ROI in healthcare, R$400k in revenue generated by a single client in one year, +13 million Brazilians reached in a national cause campaign. These results don't come from luck. They come from process.
