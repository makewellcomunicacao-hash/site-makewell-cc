Makewell Comunicação respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data, in accordance with the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (LGPD — Law No. 13,709/2018).

Contact: contato@makewell.com.br | WhatsApp

2. What data we collect

We may collect the following data when you interact with our website or contact us:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone) voluntarily provided via WhatsApp or forms

  • IP address and device/browser type

3. How we use your data

We use essential cookies for the website to function and analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our pages. You can accept or decline cookies through the banner displayed on your first visit.

If you decline, only strictly necessary cookies will be kept.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except when necessary to:

  • Use analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) — always in anonymized form

6. Your rights (LGPD)

You have the right to:

  • Confirm whether we process your personal data
  • Access, correct, or delete your data
  • Revoke consent at any time
  • Request data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contato@makewell.com.br.

7. Security

We adopt technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
